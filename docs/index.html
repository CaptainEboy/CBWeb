Who we are

Codebarrack is a tech startup that upskills Nigerian Undergraduates with various tech skills such as computer programming, coding, embedded systems etc

Our Vision

To create, nuture & grow a community of Nigerian Programmers(mostly undergraduates) who can get access to the practical aspect of coding so as to cover the lopsided theoretical aspect which is mostly offered in Nigerian univesities.

Our Mission

As a team of Electrical Electronics & Computer Engineering Undergraduates of Nigeria who are fuelled with the love & passion we have for tech and innovation. Together we believe that we and you can boost the technological sector of Nigeria. We believe in a better future, a better nation, we believe in you Nigerians.
